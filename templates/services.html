Our Services

Tyre Fitting

We offer expert tyre fitting services using precision machinery that ensures perfect installation every time. Whether you're upgrading or replacing a worn-out tyre, our trained staff handle your wheels with utmost care and accuracy.

Wheel Alignment

Accurate wheel alignment improves vehicle stability, increases tyre life, and enhances fuel efficiency. Our alignment systems use advanced sensors to restore factory-specified angles, ensuring optimal performance.

Nitrogen Air Filling

Nitrogen reduces pressure loss and enhances driving safety by maintaining consistent tyre pressure. It's ideal for long-distance travel, and we provide fast nitrogen top-ups for all tyre types.

Wheel Balancing

Unbalanced wheels can lead to vibrations, uneven wear, and suspension issues. Our digital balancing system corrects imbalance by accurately placing weights, giving you a smoother, more stable ride.

Puncture & Flat Tyre Repair

We quickly diagnose and repair punctures using professional-grade patches and plugs. Whether tubeless or tube-type, our repairs are long-lasting and thoroughly tested for safety.

Complete Wheel Care

We also provide full wheel maintenance including rim inspection, cleaning, and bolt tightening. Proper wheel care improves lifespan and prevents issues like air leakage or uneven wear.
